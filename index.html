Evaluating the Vulnerability of Rapeseed Production to Climate Change: A Case Study in the Yangtze River Basin

Posted on 2023-11-08 Edited on 2023-11-09 In Climate change , Crop model , Variety suitability
  • Accurately simulate the fertility period and yield of winter rapeseed in the Yangtze River Basin.
  • The spatial production potential of existing winter rapeseed varieties is only 1.75%.
  • Future climate change poses a greater threat to winter rapeseed production hotspots.

Overview

Rapeseed is a crucial oilseed crop in China, but its production is at risk due to climate change. This study aims to assess the planting potential and vulnerability of rapeseed production in the Yangtze River Basin, which is the key region for rapeseed cultivation in China.

Main Results

Accurately simulating crop growth and yield in the Yangtze River Basin



Distribution of suitable areas for rapeseed

Distribution of risk levels of existing varieties
