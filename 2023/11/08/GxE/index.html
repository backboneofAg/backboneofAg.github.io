Using the multi-dimensional environment zoning tool to advance crop breeding under climate change

Posted on 2023-11-08 Edited on 2023-11-09 In G×E , Climate change , Crop breeding , ML , Econometrics
  • Developed a genotype × envirotype adaptive zoning system for maize cultivation in China.
  • Interdisciplinary research by combining machine learning and econometrics in data analysis.

Overview

The success of crop breeding programs, with a purpose of either research or commercial, requires massive networks of multi-environment trials to match the most superior varieties with the target population of environments. Identifying the suitability of a variety in target regions is a costly and time-consuming process.
